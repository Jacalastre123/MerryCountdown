Help:

What to do before/after December 1st-25th:

You can costomise the page with the customise button, in it, you can change your interface colour (The header/box/popups), the background Image/Colour/Code (type BG{Any colour} for colour backgrounds or Links for my HomePage) in the textboxand choose a variety of fonts. There's secret codes that may be only seen in devTools!

What to do during December 1st-25th:

You can do the same but there will be a christmas tracker which will start at the first then ends at the 25th. There will be a status about what Santa is doing and once you click on the advent box that stands out, it will show you a fact!
